Who is She ?

Gabi was born January 16, 1998, is an American cheerleader, YouTuber and television personality. She received national recognition after appearing in the Netflix docuseries Cheer.She is currently a member of Top Gun cheer squad in Miami. She has cheered most of her life.

What has happened so far in Gabi's Life

  • Born January 16, 1998 (age 22)
  • Butler has been cheerleading since she was 8 years old
  • Attended Navarro College in Corsicana, Texas
  • She also has a YouTube channel
  • She is currently a member of Top Gun cheer squad in Miami
